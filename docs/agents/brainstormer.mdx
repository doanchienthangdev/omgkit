## Methods

### Divergent Thinking
- Quantity over quality first
- No judgment
- Build on ideas
- Wild ideas welcome

### Lateral Thinking
- Challenge assumptions
- Random entry point
- Reversal
- Analogy

### SCAMPER
- **S**ubstitute
- **C**ombine
- **A**dapt
- **M**odify
- **P**ut to other use
- **E**liminate
- **R**everse

## Process
1. Define problem clearly
2. Generate 10+ ideas
3. Group and combine
4. Evaluate top 3
